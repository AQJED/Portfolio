About Me

10+ Years Experience

I'm a Software Integration Engineer with a passion for software development, embedded systems, and AI. I hold a B.S. in Computer Engineering, specializing in high-performance and embedded systems, and a Deep Learning (AI) Nanodegree from MISK Foundation.

With over a decade in the industry, I’ve worked across various roles, from simulator technician to engineer and supervisor, gaining expertise in middleware development, real-time data integration, and system optimization. I focus on middleware solutions that connect host computers and image generators in military-grade flight simulators.

Beyond simulation, my background in embedded systems extends to microcontroller programming, circuit design, and firmware development, skills I developed both in academia and during hands-on projects. I’ve integrated hardware and software components in critical systems, improving performance and reliability.

My software skills include C/C++, Python, Matlab, Verilog, and LabVIEW, allowing me to work across embedded systems, simulation software, and AI applications. Whether it's developing real-time interfaces, optimizing embedded solutions, or implementing AI-driven automation, I thrive on solving complex technical challenges and pushing technology forward.
